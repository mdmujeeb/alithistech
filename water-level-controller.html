Smart Water Level Controller

Smart Water Level Controller is an Automatic Water Level control System which maintains level of water in your overhead water tank automatically.
The entire system can be accessed, monitored and controlled through Mobile Devices (Android) or a browser on any Computer.

Features

Smart Water Level controller, connects to the Internet through your home WiFi network.

The System can be accessed, monitored and controlled 24/7 via Android, iOS and Web Browser on any other Device or Computer.

You can monitor Water level in your Sump and Overhead water tank, Online 24/7 over the Internet, from any Corner of the World, anytime.

The System can notify you of any events of importance, immediately as they happen at your home.

You can turn on/off the Water Pump anytime from anywhere in the world over the Internet using your Mobile device or a Computer.

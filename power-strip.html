Smart WiFi 2 Independent Socket Power Strip

Connect any two devices to this Power Strip and Control them from Anywhere in the world using Internet on your Smartphone or Computer.

Connectivity

This Smart Power strip connects to Internet through your WiFi, so you can control and monitor it from anywhere in the world.

Added Manual Operation

There are 2 momentary switches, in case you want to turn on/off the sockets manually.

Schedule

You can set the schedules for each socket separately, to turn them on/off automatically at a specified time or at sunrise/sunset.

Timer Support

You can set a timer on the two sockets separately, to turn the socket on/off automatically after an interval.

Smart Occupancy Power Strip

Connect any two devices to this Power Strip and once in Auto mode, it automatically turns them on/off when there is no one in the Room. Control them from Anywhere in the world using Internet on your Smartphone or Computer.

Occupancy Sensing

When Auto mode is turned on, it automatically turns the devices on/off when there is someone in the Room.

Connectivity

This Smart Power strip connects to Internet through your WiFi, so you can control and monitor it from anywhere in the world.

Schedule
+ Manual Operation

You can set the schedules for each socket separately, to turn them on/off automatically at a specified time or at sunrise / sunset. There are 2 momentary switches, in case you want to turn on/off the sockets manually.

Timer Support

You can set a timer on the two sockets separately, to turn the socket on/off automatically after an interval.

Smart, Automatic Plant Watering System

Set the time in the system, for morning & evening at which you usually water your Plants, and forget worrying about watering your plants again. This system regularly, automatically waters your plants daily.

Connectivity

This System connects to Internet through your WiFi, so you can control and monitor it from anywhere in the world.

Schedule

You can set multiple schedules for watering your plants throughout the day, and specified days in week, for specified time, or sunrise/sunset.

Timer Support

You can also manually turn on the watering, and set a timer to automatically turn it off after specified duration.

Configuration

Water Terminals for each Plant

A single high capacity water pipe is circulated around all your plants with Terminals ending with each plant.

Controller Device

The Central Controller Device connects to Internet through your WiFi, and controls watering of the plants through Automatic Water flow valve. You can access this device from your Smartphone or PC through the internet.

Automatic Water flow Valve

The main water pipe is connected to your water supply, through this Automatic Water flow valve, which is controlled by the central Controller Device.
